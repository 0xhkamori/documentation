<details>
    <summary>Problems and solutions</summary>

    ## Microphone
    **Problem**: Microphone does not work in any apps

    **Solution:** Flashing [Android Mic Fix](https://xdaforums.com/t/module-android-mic-fix.3503128/) via Magisk

    ## Statusbar
    **Problem:** Statusbar is incorrectly positioned

    **Solution**: Installing [Iconify](https://github.com/Mahmud0808/Iconify) and adjusting its position

    ## Charging
    **Problem:** If you charge the phone while it is turned off, it will turn on but will not be able to boot
